% Beyond the default SparseLDA sweep, \pkg{topica} exposes the LDA inference
% engine as a pluggable backend through the \code{sampler} argument, behind one
% fitted-model contract. \code{"warp"} is the cache-efficient WarpLDA sampler
% \citep{chen2016warplda}, an $O(1)$-per-token Metropolis--Hastings scheme whose
% per-sweep cost is flat in $K$; at the fine-grained topic counts common in
% social science (e.g.\ $K=1{,}000$) it fits several times faster than SparseLDA
% and reaches higher coherence. \code{"cvb0"} is collapsed variational Bayes,
% zeroth-order \citep{asuncion2009smoothing}, a deterministic, non-sampling
% alternative that trades speed for higher topic coherence and exact
% reproducibility. The same backends extend to the per-document-prior
% (\code{DMR}) and seeded (\code{SeededLDA}) models, since they share the
% count-based collapsed conditional.
